Weapon Modifications

Adding a weapon attachment to a weapon costs 30 gold. A weapon can have one attachment for each customization part. Modifications on this table can be added to weapons with the Fabrication skill.

Masterwork Weapons

Masterwork weapons cost an additional 300 gold and gain one modification from this table. Each weapon can have 1 Masterwork Modification, but it must be of a type for which the weapon has a modification slot (as listed below). Importantly, Masterwork Modifications do not take the place of a regular weapon attachment. Masterwork modifications cannot be added to existing weapons, but must be added at the time of their creation. All masterwork weapons have a rarity 1 higher than a regular weapon of their type, with a minimum of 4.
